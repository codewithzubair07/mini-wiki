# Vannevar Bush

## Summary
Vannevar Bush (1890–1974) was an American engineer, inventor, and science administrator. He is best known in the context of personal knowledge management for his 1945 essay *As We May Think*, in which he described the **Memex** — a hypothetical personal knowledge device that prefigured the web and the idea of personal wikis.

## Key Facts
- Described the Memex in his 1945 essay *As We May Think* (published in The Atlantic)
- The Memex concept: a personal, curated knowledge store with associative trails between documents
- His vision was closer to a privately maintained, richly cross-referenced knowledge base than to what the web became
- The part he couldn't solve was **who does the maintenance** — the LLM handles that
